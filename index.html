Dog Care Tips

  • Feed them high-quality dog food twice a day.
  • Walk your dog at least once daily.
  • Brush their teeth and coat regularly.

Cat Care Tips

  • Provide fresh water and balanced meals.
  • Keep a clean litter box.
  • Offer scratching posts and toys.

Bird Care Tips

  • Clean the cage weekly.
  • Offer fresh fruits and veggies.
  • Spend time socializing with them daily.
